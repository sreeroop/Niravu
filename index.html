ABOUT US

It was in November 1, 2006 (Kerala formation day) that the Niravu Residents Association, Vengeri in Kozhikode City corporation decided to actively take to organic farming and ensure that every member household had a backyard vegetable garden for them. Niravu had as many as 101 residents then. When the forum came across a scarcity of good and indigenous vegetable seeds, its members decided to collect vegetable seeds from different parts of the State.
